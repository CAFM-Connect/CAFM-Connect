Introduction

The Open-BIM interface for the exchange of real estate data - Planning, building, operating with one data standard

A clean and complete overview of a building's data is essential for safe and legally compliant operation. Before the building is completed, it is planned and built. During the planning and construction phase, two scenarios often arise: Building data is recorded that cannot be automatically used for the subsequent software system in operation (CAFM system) or no data is recorded at all. Result: Data must now be entered manually, which costs time and money. An annoyance that can be avoided: with CAFM-Connect.

CAFM-Connect is an initiative of associations in the real estate industry that are committed to the digitalisation of real estate operations and have created a uniform and open data standard based on IFC to simplify cooperation in the industry.

CAFM Connect consists of :

BIM Profiles

  • Data exchange standards for BIM use cases in building operations
  • Contractual basis for customer information requirements (AiA)
  • Published by experts with a high level of knowledge about the respective application

CAFM Connect Editor

  • Software program for recording buildings and their components and documents on the basis of BIM profiles
  • Usability in compatible CAFM systems
  • Usable without installation as viewer or editor on every workstation
  • Free of charge
