1.1.1 1 - Data template exchange

ProcessSenderReceiver
31-10 00 00 Inception Phase

The "Data template exchange" defines the structure of templates that can be used for the definition of the information structure for construction objects to be exchanged. Data templates essentially contain the definitions of characteristics and their grouping for a specific purpose. This standard does not specify specific property definitions, but rather provides the basis for stakeholders to agree among themselves to exchange property definitions and their grouping.

The content of the data templates shall be loadable from a template based on EN ISO/DIS 23386 and ISO/DIS 23387.
